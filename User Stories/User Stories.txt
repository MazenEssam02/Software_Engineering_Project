User Stories
1. As a user, I want to add a new expense with the amount, date, and a short description so I can track my spending.

2. As a user, I want to categorize my expenses (e.g., groceries, rent, entertainment) to understand my spending habits better.

3. As a user, I want to edit or delete an existing expense to maintain accurate records.

4. As a user, I want to set a budget for different categories or overall spending to stay financially conscious.

5. As a user, I want to view my remaining budget for each category to see how much I can still spend.

6. As a user, I want to generate reports on my spending history by category, date range, or other filters for financial analysis.

7. As a user, I want to view reports visually (e.g., charts, graphs) to easily understand my spending patterns.

8. As a user, I want to import my bank transactions (if supported by the bank) to save time on manual entry.

9. As a user, I want to add recurring expenses (e.g., monthly bills) for automatic tracking.

10. As a user, I want to receive notifications when I am approaching or exceeding a budget limit.

11. As a user, I want to set financial goals (e.g., saving for a vacation) and track my progress.

12. As a user, I want to add notes to specific transactions for additional context.

13. As a user, I want to search for specific transactions by amount, category, or keyword in the description.

14. As a user, I want to export my expense data to a spreadsheet or other format for further analysis.

15. As a user, I want the app to be secure and protect my financial data with encryption and authentication.

4. As a business owner, I want to import bank statements directly into the app to avoid manual data entry.

5. As a parent, I want to receive notifications when my child exceeds their allocated pocket money budget.

6. As a student, I want to attach images of receipts to transactions for easy reference.

7. As a retiree, I want to generate expense reports for tax purposes.

8. As a frequent shopper, I want to set spending limits for specific stores or online platforms.

9. As a user concerned about privacy, I want the app to encrypt my financial data to ensure security.

10. As a traveler, I want the app to support multiple currencies for accurate expense tracking during trips.

11. As a busy professional, I want the app's user interface to be intuitive and clutter-free for quick expense entry.

12. As a user with visual impairments, I want the app to be accessible with screen readers and voice commands.

13. As a user, I want the app to sync data across devices (e.g., phone, tablet, web) for seamless usage.

15. As a user, I want the app to provide data backup and recovery options to prevent data loss.

16.As a user, I want to take a picture of my receipt or upload an image to easily track expenses without manual entry.

17.As a user, I want to add and manage different payment methods (e.g., cash, credit card, debit card) to categorize my spending accordingly.

18.As a user, I want to log in securely with a strong password or biometric authentication (e.g., fingerprint) to protect my financial data.

19.As a user, I want to be able to enter expenses and manage basic functionalities even when I'm offline. The data should sync automatically when I regain internet connection.

20.As a user, I want to personalize the app's interface with different themes and layouts to suit my preferences.

21.As a user, I want to export my reports as PDFs or CSV files to share them with my financial advisor or for further analysis.

22.As a user, I want to view a list of all my transactions, filtered by date, category, or keyword for easy reference.
