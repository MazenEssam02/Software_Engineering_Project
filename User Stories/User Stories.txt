User Stories
As a user, I want to log in to my account using my username or email address and password to access my expense data.

As a user, I want to recover my account password if I forget it

As a user, I want to add a new expense with the amount, date, and a short description so I can track my spending.

As a user, I want to categorize my expenses (e.g., groceries, rent, entertainment) to understand my spending habits better.

As a user, I want to edit or delete an existing expense to maintain accurate records.

As a user, I want to set a budget for different categories or overall spending to stay financially conscious.

As a user, I want to view my remaining budget for each category to see how much I can still spend.

As a user, I want to generate reports on my spending history by category, date range, or other filters for financial analysis.

As a user, I want to view reports visually (e.g., charts, graphs) to easily understand my spending patterns.

As a user, I want to import my bank transactions (if supported by the bank) to save time on manual entry.

As a user, I want to add recurring expenses (e.g., monthly bills) for automatic tracking.

As a user, I want to receive notifications when I am approaching or exceeding a budget limit.

As a user, I want to set financial goals (e.g., saving for a vacation) and track my progress.

As a user, I want to add notes to specific transactions for additional context.

As a user, I want to export my expense data to a spreadsheet or other format for further analysis.

As a user, I want the app to be secure and protect my financial data with encryption and authentication.

As a business owner, I want to import bank statements directly into the app to avoid manual data entry.

As a parent, I want to receive notifications when my child exceeds their allocated pocket money budget.

As a student, I want to attach images of receipts to transactions for easy reference.

As a retiree, I want to generate expense reports for tax purposes.

As a user concerned about privacy, I want the app to encrypt my financial data to ensure security.

As a traveler, I want the app to support multiple currencies for accurate expense tracking during trips.

As a user, I want the app to sync data across devices (e.g., phone, tablet, web) for seamless usage.

As a user, I want the app to provide data backup and recovery options to prevent data loss.

As a user, I want to take a picture of my receipt or upload an image to easily track expenses without manual entry.

As a user, I want to add and manage different payment methods (e.g., cash, credit card, debit card) to categorize my spending accordingly.

As a user, I want to be able to enter expenses and manage basic functionalities even when I'm offline. The data should sync automatically when I regain internet connection.

As a user, I want to set reminders for upcoming bills or expenses to avoid late fees and penalties.

As a user, I want to view a list of all my transactions, filtered by date, category, or keyword for easy reference.
